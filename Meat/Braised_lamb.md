#Orange's Braised Lamb

##Ingredients
###Fresh
* (3 lbs) Lamb
* Ginger (about 5g sliced)
* Garlic (about 5g sliced)

###Spices
* (1pc) Cinnamon stick
* (2pc) Bay leaf
* (2pc) Star anise
* (4pc) Dried whole hot peppers

###Sauces and etc
* (1/3 cc) Soy sauce
* (1 tbsp) Cooking wine (mirin, sake, wine, Shaoxin etc.)
* (2 tbsp) Sugar (can use honey, brown sugar)
* (3 tsp) Salt
* (1 cc) Water
